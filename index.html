My Music

This music app allows you to customize the color theme, enjoy your favorite songs by pressing favorites, you can create the playlist, add the name, show the date the playlist was created, and add the songs, adjust the equalizer and share a song with your friends.

My Music features:

- Animations on click.

- Create playlist.

- Play songs in the album.

- Share songs.

- Customize interface.

- Supported music and audio format: MP3, WMA, WAV, M4A, etc.

- Press Favorites if you like the song.

- Equalizer.

Instructions: - Click on the Search icon.

My Video

This video app, play your favorite videos, customize the color theme, support MP4, MOV, AVI, etc. video formats. and you can share videos.

My Video Features:

- Animations on click.

- Video folders.

- Share video.

- Customize interface.

- Supported video format: MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, etc.

- Brightness adjustment.

- Speed adjustment.

- PIP mode.

Instructions:

- Keep pressing a video for more options.

- double press right to fast forward or left to rewind.

- swipe up or down to control brightness.
